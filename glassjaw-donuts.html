About Glassjaw

We started Glassjaw because the donut scene needed a punch in the face. No drive-thru shortcuts, no pre-made glazes, no apologizing for what a donut can be. Just the best ingredients we can find, scratch-made dough, and an honest hour of work for every batch. Try one. You'll see.
